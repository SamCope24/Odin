Pizza

Description

Pizza is the best takeaway option without a doubt. It is also incredibly easy to make at home. Feel free to go crazy with the toppings... just remember to not put any pineapple on there!

This page will teach you how to make a really simple yet tasty pizza!

Ingredients

  • Flour and yeast for the base
  • Some kind of tomato based sauce
  • A cheese that melts well like mozzarella
  • Any other toppings you like (No pineapple)

How to Prepare

  1. Preheat the oven to mega hot
  2. Mix all the dough ingredients than whack in the fridge to rise
  3. Stretch out the dough and slap on the sauce
  4. Load it up with toppings + your cheese of choice
  5. Throw it in the oven for 15 mins
  6. Enjoy!
