Stew

Description

Stew is a great meal to make if you're on a budget and also have a lot of leftover veg lying around the house.

In just a few hours you can make a really nice meal with minimal effort. This page will show you how to do it!

Ingredients

  • Some kind of meat
  • Some veggies
  • Some seasoning
  • Bread for the side

How to Prepare

  1. Put a pan on the heat
  2. Brown the meat
  3. Chuck in the veggies
  4. Throw in some stock and put the lid on
  5. Cook until you can't wait any longer
  6. Enjoy!
